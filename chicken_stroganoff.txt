Ingredients:
3 chicken breasts cut into cubes
1 clove garlic, minced
Salt and pepper to taste
1 chopped onion
2 tablespoons mayonnaise
1 spoon of butter
1/2 cup ketchup
1/3 glass of mustard
1 glass of mushrooms
1 glass of sour cream
Potato sticks

Directions:
In a saucepan, combine the chicken, garlic, mayonnaise, salt and pepper
In a large frying pan, melt the butter and brown the onion
Add the seasoned chicken until golden
Add mushrooms, ketchup and mustard
Incorporate the cream and remove from heat before boiling
Serve with white rice and straw potatoes
